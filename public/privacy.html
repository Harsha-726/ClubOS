Privacy Policy

Last updated: beta period, subject to change
Beta-period draft, written to be genuinely accurate about what the app does today — not a substitute for real legal review, especially if minors' data is involved (school clubs, for example). Get real legal advice before wide release.

What we collect

  • From Clerk (sign-in): your name and email, to identify you and match you to club rosters.
  • From you or your club's officers: phone number and emergency contact (optional), grade/year, attendance records, payment status for club dues, and anything you post as an announcement.
  • From Stripe (if your club uses it): we never see or store your card details — Stripe handles that under its own privacy policy. We only see whether a payment succeeded.

Who can see it

Officers of your specific club can see your roster information — name, email, phone, attendance, payment status. Regular members see their own data and whatever officers share (the roster, announcements, deadlines). Different clubs on this platform cannot see each other's data — that separation is enforced at the database level, not just hidden in the interface.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. We don't use it for anything beyond running the features of the app itself.

Where it's stored

Club data lives in a Postgres database hosted by Supabase. Identity and authentication are handled by Clerk. Both are third-party infrastructure providers with their own security practices and their own privacy policies governing how they handle data on our behalf.

Minors

If this is used by a school club, some members may be under 18. We don't currently have a separate parental-consent flow — if you're a school administrator or parent with concerns about a specific club's use of this app, contact that club's officers directly, or reach us through the feedback option in the app.

Your choices

  • You can leave a club yourself at any time (Profile page) — this removes you from that club's roster and data.
  • You can ask a club's President to delete the entire club, which erases everyone's data for that club.
  • You can ask us directly to delete your account and data if you can't do it yourself through the app.

Contact

Use "Send Feedback" in the app for anything privacy-related, and we'll respond directly.
