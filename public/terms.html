What this is

Club OS is software for running a club — tracking members, deadlines, dues, and announcements. It's currently in testing. Things may change, break, or reset without much notice while that's true.

Your account

Signing in is handled by Clerk, a third-party identity provider — we never see or store your password. You're responsible for keeping your own account secure.

Club data

Anything you or your club's officers enter — member names, emails, attendance, payment status, announcements — is stored to run the app's features. Officers you promote can see and manage this data for their club; regular members can see their own information and whatever officers choose to share (announcements, deadlines, the roster).

Payments

Dues payments are processed by Stripe. We don't store your card details — Stripe does, under their own terms. During the beta, all clubs' dues flow through a single connected Stripe account; this is expected to change before real, unrelated clubs are onboarded at scale.

No warranty, beta software

This is provided as-is, during an active testing period. We don't guarantee uptime, that data won't be lost, or that every feature works exactly as intended. Please don't treat this as your club's only record of anything financially or organizationally critical yet.

Leaving / deleting your data

You can leave a club at any time from your Profile page. A club's President can permanently delete the club and all its data from the Reports page. If you want your personal data removed and can't do it yourself through the app, contact us and we'll handle it directly.

Contact

Questions or concerns — use the "Send Feedback" option in the app, or reach out directly if you know how to contact us outside it.
